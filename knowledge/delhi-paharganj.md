# Area Profile: Paharganj, Delhi

> **Source:** Survey of 11 Delhi locals. All scores are on a 1–5 scale and come only from this sample.

## Snapshot

- **Night safety (avg):** 1.3 / 5 → **low perceived safety**
- **Noise at night:** 1.5 / 5 → **noisy**
- **Late-night food variety:** 2.5 / 5 → **limited late-night options**
- **Cabs/autos hard at odd hours:** mentioned in **7 / 11** responses
- **Nightlife mentions:** **1 / 11** associated it with nightlife

## How locals seem to see Paharganj

From this sample:

- Paharganj is perceived as **one of the less safe areas at night**.
- Locals see it as **quite noisy**, not ideal if someone wants peaceful sleep.
- Cabs/autos at odd hours are a **common pain point** (flagged by most respondents).
- Food variety late at night is there, but not rated very highly.
- A few people associate it with nightlife, but it is **not a top nightlife pick** in this survey.
