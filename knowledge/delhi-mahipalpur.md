# Area Profile: Mahipalpur, Delhi

> **Source:** Survey of 11 Delhi locals. All scores are on a 1–5 scale and come only from this sample.

## Snapshot

- **Night safety (avg):** 1.7 / 5 → **low perceived safety**
- **Noise at night (sleep quality):** 2.1 / 5 → **somewhat noisy**
- **Late-night food variety:** 2.4 / 5 → **limited late-night options**
- **Cabs/autos hard at odd hours:** mentioned in **5 / 11** responses
- **Nightlife mentions:** **0 / 11** picked it as a “vibrant nightlife” area

## How locals seem to see Mahipalpur (from the survey)

In this small sample, Mahipalpur comes across as:

- More of a **functional / airport-adjacent stay** than a comfortable leisure base.
- **Safety scores are low**, and several people say cabs and autos can be harder to find late at night.
- Noise scores suggest it may not be the quietest choice for light sleepers.
- Late-night food is **not completely dead**, but variety feels limited.
