# Area Profile: GK / Defence Colony / Green Park / Nehru Place (cluster), Delhi

> **Source:** Survey of 11 Delhi locals. All scores are on a 1–5 scale and come only from this sample.  
> The survey treated GK, Defence Colony, Green Park and Nehru Place as **one combined area**.

## Snapshot

- **Night safety (avg):** 3.8 / 5 → **above-average perceived safety**
- **Noise at night:** 3.8 / 5 → **moderately quiet to fairly quiet**
- **Late-night food variety:** 4.0 / 5 → **many late-night options**
- **Cabs/autos hard at odd hours:** **0 / 11** flagged this cluster
- **Nightlife mentions:** **10 / 11** picked this cluster as “vibrant nightlife”

## How locals seem to see this GK/DefCol/Green Park/Nehru cluster

From this small sample:

- This cluster is seen as **relatively safe at night** compared to other areas in the survey.
- Noise ratings suggest it is **fairly comfortable to sleep in**, not as loud as the very busy hubs.
- Late-night food and delivery get a **strong rating** — lots of perceived variety.
- No one said cabs/autos were hard to get here at odd hours, which suggests **good connectivity**.
- It is the **top nightlife cluster** in the sample; almost everyone picked it for nightlife.
