# Area Profile: Dwarka, Delhi

> **Source:** Survey of 11 Delhi locals. All scores are on a 1–5 scale and come only from this sample.

## Snapshot

- **Night safety (avg):** 2.4 / 5 → **mixed perceived safety**
- **Noise at night:** 3.1 / 5 → **moderately quiet**
- **Late-night food variety:** 2.9 / 5 → **limited to okay late-night options**
- **Cabs/autos hard at odd hours:** mentioned in **7 / 11** responses
- **Nightlife mentions:** **0 / 11** picked it as a nightlife area

## How locals seem to see Dwarka

From this small dataset:

- Safety perceptions are **mixed** — not the worst, but not strongly positive either.
- Quietness scores are **slightly on the quieter side**, which can help sleep.
- Late-night food options are seen as **average at best**.
- A large portion of respondents (**7 of 11**) said cabs/autos are hard to find at odd hours, which is a big practical point for late arrivals.
- No one picked it as a nightlife spot.
