# Area Profile: Connaught Place (CP), Delhi

> **Source:** Survey of 11 Delhi locals. All scores are on a 1–5 scale and come only from this sample.

## Snapshot

- **Night safety (avg):** 3.4 / 5 → **above-average perceived safety**
- **Noise at night:** 2.6 / 5 → **somewhat noisy**
- **Late-night food variety:** 4.0 / 5 → **many late-night options**
- **Cabs/autos hard at odd hours:** mentioned in **1 / 11** responses
- **Nightlife mentions:** **8 / 11** picked CP as “vibrant nightlife”

## How locals seem to see Connaught Place

According to the survey:

- CP is seen as **fairly safe at night**, but also **busy and somewhat noisy**, which matches its role as a central hub.
- Late-night food is a **strong point** — variety and availability rated highly.
- Only one person flagged it as hard for cabs/autos at odd hours, which suggests **good overall connectivity**.
- It’s clearly seen as a **major nightlife centre**, with most respondents picking it.
