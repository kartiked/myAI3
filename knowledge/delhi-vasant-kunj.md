# Area Profile: Vasant Kunj, Delhi

> **Source:** Survey of 11 Delhi locals. All scores are on a 1–5 scale and come only from this sample.

## Snapshot

- **Night safety (avg):** 3.2 / 5 → **above-average perceived safety**
- **Noise at night:** 3.6 / 5 → **moderately quiet**
- **Late-night food variety:** 3.5 / 5 → **decent late-night options**
- **Cabs/autos hard at odd hours:** mentioned in **3 / 11** responses
- **Nightlife mentions:** **4 / 11** associated it with nightlife

## How locals seem to see Vasant Kunj

In this sample:

- Safety is seen as **reasonably good**, better than some cheaper areas.
- Quietness is **moderately good**, which may suit families or people who want calmer nights.
- Late-night food is seen as **decent** — not top tier like Aerocity/GK cluster, but not dead either.
- A minority of respondents said cabs/autos can be harder to find late at night.
- A few people linked it to **some nightlife**, but it’s not a primary nightlife hub in this dataset.
